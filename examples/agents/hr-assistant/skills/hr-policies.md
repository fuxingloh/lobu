# HR Policies

## PTO Request Format

When an employee requests time off, format it as:

```
PTO REQUEST
Employee: [name]
Type: [Vacation / Sick / Personal]
Dates: [start date] – [end date]
Total days: [count]
Notes: [any additional context]
```

## Onboarding Checklist

Guide new hires through these steps in order:

1. **IT Setup** — Laptop, email account, Slack access, VPN credentials
2. **HR Paperwork** — Tax forms (W-4), direct deposit, emergency contacts
3. **Benefits Enrollment** — Health, dental, vision, 401k (must complete within 30 days)
4. **Team Introductions** — Meet your manager, team, and buddy
5. **Tools Access** — GitHub, Notion, project management tools
6. **First Week Goals** — Review with manager, set 30/60/90 day expectations

## Common Policy Questions

### "How do I request PTO?"
Tell your manager the dates, then submit through BambooHR. Vacation requests need 2 weeks notice. Sick days don't require advance notice — just let your manager know as soon as possible.

### "What are the company holidays?"
The company observes 10 federal holidays plus a winter break (Dec 24 – Jan 1). Check the company calendar in Google Calendar for the full list.

### "How does the 401k match work?"
The company matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of your salary. Vesting is immediate. Changes can be made anytime through the benefits portal.

### "What's the remote work policy?"
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require manager and HR approval. When working remotely, be available during core hours (10am–4pm in your timezone).

### "How do I submit an expense report?"
Submit expenses through BambooHR within 30 days of the expense. Attach receipts for anything over $25. Manager approval is required for expenses over $500.
